The creation assistant guides you through stringing your hoop one segment at a time. Instead of trying to follow a long list of numbers, you follow a live canvas that always shows exactly where to go next.

<Note>
  The assistant reads the pin sequence directly from the textarea. Make sure your sequence is present before clicking **Start Creating**. See [Pin sequence output](/guides/pin-sequence-output) if you need to restore a saved sequence.
</Note>

## How it works

When you click **Start Creating**, an incremental drawing panel appears. The canvas updates as you advance through each step:

- **Current line** — drawn in **red**, with a line width of 1. This is the segment you need to string right now.
- **All previous lines** — drawn in black, with a thinner line width of 0.3, so the overall pattern remains visible.
- **Status text** — shows `Current Line: N | Pin A to B`, telling you the step number and exactly which two pins the current segment connects.

## Navigating steps

| Action | Controls |
|--------|----------|
| Advance one step | **Next Step** button, **spacebar**, or **right arrow key** |
| Go back one step | **Last Step** button or **left arrow key** |

<Info>
  The red highlight on the current line makes it easy to see where to go next, even when working with a large hoop that has many closely spaced pins.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Going back one step redraws all previous lines from scratch. At high step counts (thousands of lines), this can take a noticeable amount of time.
</Warning>

## Tips for physical stringing

<Tip>
  Number your pins with a marker before you start stringing. The assistant always tells you the destination pin number, so labeled pins let you find the right nail instantly without counting.
</Tip>

## Just Draw mode

If you do not need step-by-step guidance — for example, if you just want to preview the finished result — click **Just Draw** instead of **Start Creating**. This skips the assistant entirely and renders the full sequence on the canvas in one pass.
